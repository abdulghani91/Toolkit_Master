Privacy Policy

ToolKit Master

Last Updated: June 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how ToolKit Master ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect several types of information from and about users of our App:

a. Device Information

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address

b. Usage Data

  • App interactions (e.g., which tools are opened, how often)
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Session duration and navigation paths

c. Sensor Data (Processed Locally)

  • Accelerometer data: Used for the Bubble Level tool.
  • Magnetic field data: Used for the Compass tool.
  • Microphone audio: Used for the Sound Meter tool to calculate decibel levels.

Important: All sensor data is processed locally on your device in real-time. We do not record, store, or transmit any audio, video, or sensor readings to our servers.

d. AdMob Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. The ad formats used are Banner ads (bottom of tool screens) and Interstitial ads (full-screen, shown every few tool opens). AdMob may collect and process data as described in Google's privacy policies, including:

  • Approximate location (based on IP)
  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • App interactions with ads

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob Terms of Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain the App
  • To improve the user experience and develop new tools
  • To deliver personalized and non-personalized advertisements via AdMob
  • To troubleshoot crashes and technical issues
  • To analyze aggregated usage trends

3. Data Storage and Security

All calculation data, history, and preferences are stored locally on your device using SQLite and SharedPreferences. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

We take reasonable measures to protect the security of your information, but please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With Google (AdMob) for advertising purposes
  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party SDKs and services:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): For in-app advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Frankfurter API: Used for live currency exchange rates. This service does not collect personal data. Frankfurter
  • Google Fonts: Fonts loaded from Google's CDN may trigger logging. Privacy Policy

6. Permissions Explained

The App requests the following Android permissions, and each is strictly limited to its purpose:

  • INTERNET — Required for fetching live currency rates and loading ads.
  • RECORD_AUDIO — Required only for the Sound Meter tool. Audio data is processed in real-time; nothing is recorded or saved.
  • CAMERA — Required only for the Flashlight tool on certain devices that utilize camera flash hardware.
  • FLASHLIGHT — Required to control the device torch.
  • VIBRATE — Required for the Vibration Test tool.
  • android.hardware.sensor.compass — Declared as optional for the Compass tool.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Choices

  • Opt-out of personalized ads: You can reset your Advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings -> Google -> Ads).
  • Permission removal: You can revoke permissions at any time via your device's app settings. Note that some features will stop functioning if required permissions are revoked.
  • Uninstall: You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the App from your device.

9. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device (history, favorites, settings, cached currency rates) persists until you uninstall the App or clear the App's data via system settings. Analytics and ad-related data held by third parties (Google) is governed by their respective retention policies.

10. International Data Transfers

Some third-party services (e.g., Google AdMob) may process data outside your country of residence. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of information to countries outside your jurisdiction.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically for any changes.
